﻿# ARMORER

> **Level 5 Valor Ability**  
> **Recall Cost:** 1

While you’re wearing armor, gain a +1 bonus to your Armor Score.

During a rest, when you choose to repair your armor as a downtime move, your allies also clear an Armor Slot.
